Homework 7



As a European Studies Major and as a Turkish-American I am deeply intrested in Turkish politics, as such, for this project I decided to create both a cloropleth and a proportional symbol map showcasing the results of the 2023 Turkish Presidential Election. I acquired the data for this project from the website of the Turkish Supreme Electoral Board (YSK). The data I aquired only showcased the total amount of votes recieved by candidate in each province. While creating my cleaned CSV, I added up the votes all candidates recieved by province to calculate the total votes cast in each province. This, in turn, allowed me to calculate the precentage of votes recieved by each candidate in each province which I then used to create my coloropleth. I created the coloropleth using two different layers; one layer using the vote precentage of Erdoğan and the other using that of Kılıçdaroğlu. For both candidates I creates six breaks (0-40, 40-45, 45-50, 50-55, 55-60, and 60-100), placing most of the emphasis on swing provinces while creating less distinction between provinces safely won by one party. I placed the layer for Kılıçdaroğlu above that for Erdoğan and hid the provinces where Kılıçdaroğlurecıeved less than 40% of the voteö which allowed for the Erdoğan layer to be visible from underneath. Due to the fact that there were two third-party candidates there were also party challengers I had to account for provinces where the candidate that recieved the most recieved less than a majority. To do acomplish this I used the rules based formating option to specificy that for the Kılıçdaroğlu layer objects in the 45-50 category had to be both between 45 and 50 AND that the Kılıçdaroğlu vote had to be larger than the Erdoğan vote share. Additionally, for the pie chart I decided to make the pie chart of each province proportional to the amount of votes cast in that province.
